Project 1: Homepage Class Portfolio

Description

Building off of Exercise 0: git init, you will use HTML/CSS/JS to create your own homepage that will contain links to your exercises, projects, and any other media/materials you so choose. Be playful, expressive, and experimental. Write a bio introducing yourself. Add text, images, sound, and interactions. It is your site and you may represent yourself and your work however you choose!
